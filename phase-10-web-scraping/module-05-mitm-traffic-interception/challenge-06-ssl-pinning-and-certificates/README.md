# Challenge: SSL Pinning and Certificates

## What you'll learn
Why some apps resist MITM interception, how SSL/TLS pinning works, and what certificates do in the MITM context.

## Watch first
- [SSL/TLS Explained in 5 Minutes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0TLDTodL7Lc) (5 min) — How HTTPS and certificates work at a high level

## Open Claude Code and say:
> "I'm ready for the challenge"

## You're done when
- [ ] You can explain what SSL/TLS certificates do and why they matter
- [ ] You understand how mitmproxy's CA certificate lets it intercept HTTPS traffic
- [ ] You can explain what certificate pinning is and why apps use it
- [ ] You understand why some traffic cannot be intercepted even with mitmproxy
