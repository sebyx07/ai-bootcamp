# Challenge: What Is a Terminal?

## What you'll learn
What a terminal is, why developers use it, and how to open one on your computer.

## Watch first
- [The 50 Most Popular Linux & Terminal Commands](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZtqBQ68cfJc) (5 hr, watch first 10 min) — Colt Steele explains what the terminal is and why it matters
- [Linux for Hackers EP 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VbEx7B_PTOE) (20 min) — NetworkChuck's fun, beginner-friendly intro to Linux

## Open Claude Code and say:
> "I'm ready for the challenge"

## You're done when
- [ ] You can explain what a terminal is in your own words
- [ ] You opened a terminal on your computer
- [ ] You typed a command and saw output
- [ ] You know the difference between a terminal and a graphical interface
