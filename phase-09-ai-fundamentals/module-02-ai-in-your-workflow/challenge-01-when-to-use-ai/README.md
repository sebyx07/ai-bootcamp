# Challenge: When to Use AI

## What you'll learn
How to develop judgment about when AI accelerates your work and when it slows you down or leads you astray.

## Watch first
- [How I Use LLMs — Andrej Karpathy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EWvNQjAaOHw) (131 min) — Practical guide from an AI expert on when and how he uses LLMs in his own work (watch the first 30 min for key insights)

## Open Claude Code and say:
> "I'm ready for the challenge"

## You're done when
- [ ] You can list five tasks where AI excels and five where it struggles
- [ ] You have a personal decision framework for when to use AI vs do it yourself
- [ ] You understand the concept of "AI-appropriate tasks"
- [ ] You reflected on your own AI usage patterns and identified improvements
