# Challenge: Limitations and Ethics

## What you'll learn
The ethical considerations of using AI: bias in AI systems, when NOT to use AI, over-reliance risks, and your responsibility as an AI user.

## Watch first
- [How I'm fighting bias in algorithms — Joy Buolamwini (TED)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UG_X_7g63rY) (9 min) — MIT researcher reveals how facial recognition fails on darker-skinned faces, exposing AI bias
- [How to keep human bias out of AI — Kriti Sharma (TED)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JkJBRjFkbKI) (12 min) — Practical talk on how human bias enters AI systems and three ways to build more ethical AI

## Open Claude Code and say:
> "I'm ready for the challenge"

## You're done when
- [ ] You can explain how bias enters AI systems
- [ ] You can list three situations where you should NOT use AI
- [ ] You understand the risks of over-reliance on AI
- [ ] You have formed your own views on responsible AI use
