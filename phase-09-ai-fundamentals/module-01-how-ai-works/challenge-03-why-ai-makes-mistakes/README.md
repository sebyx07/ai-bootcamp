# Challenge: Why AI Makes Mistakes

## What you'll learn
Why AI models hallucinate, when to trust AI output, when to verify it, and how to develop healthy skepticism without losing trust.

## Watch first
- [Intro to Large Language Models — Andrej Karpathy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zjkBMFhNj_g) (60 min) — Covers LLM limitations, security, and failure modes in the second half

## Open Claude Code and say:
> "I'm ready for the challenge"

## You're done when
- [ ] You can explain what "hallucination" means in AI
- [ ] You understand why LLMs confidently say wrong things
- [ ] You have a personal checklist for when to verify AI output
- [ ] You know the types of tasks where AI is most and least reliable
